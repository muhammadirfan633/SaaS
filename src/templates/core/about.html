About TechHub

TechHub is a small, developer-focused platform that combines practical tutorials, online tools, and custom development services. The goal is simple: help students, solo developers, and small teams build and automate real projects with clear guidance.

What TechHub offers

  • Step‑by‑step tutorials on Python, Django, web scraping, and web development
  • Free online tools for working with text, files, and (soon) automated QA workflows
  • Custom development services for websites, dashboards, and automation scripts

Who is behind TechHub?

TechHub is created by a university student and Python/Django developer from Lahore who enjoys building useful tools, automating repetitive tasks, and explaining complex topics in a simple way for learners and small business owners.

If you want to collaborate, suggest a tutorial, or discuss a project, you can reach out anytime through the contact page.
